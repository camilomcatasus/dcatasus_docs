2 Navigate to Settings (Phone Only)

There are two ways to navigate to the settings menu:

  1. From the homepage, click on the gear icon for the desired Camera
  2. Navigate to the Camera's page by clicking on the video, then click on the gear icon

3 User Management

3.1 Desktop

To navigate to the User Management section:*

  1. Click on the settings button shaped like a gear
  2. Navigate to the System section on the left hand side and click it to expand the list.
  3. Click on User Management and a view will appear of all existing users.

To add, edit or delete users:

  • To add a user, click on the Add User button, then input the necessary information.
  • To delete a user, click on the trash icon to the right of the user. Then confirm the deletion
  • To edit a user, click on the edit button next to the trash icon of the specific user you want to edit.

3.2 Phone

To navigate to the User Management section and add users:

  1. Scroll down in the settings until you see the Advanced option
  2. Click on User Management
  3. Click on either
    • Add User to add a user that can't change device settings
    • Add Admin to add a user that can change device settings
  4. Enter user details
  5. Click Save

4 Changing Push Notification Schedule

The gray sections of the schedule mean that the application will not send notification during that time, the blue squares indicate that the application will send notifications.

4.1 Desktop

To navigate to the section of the application where you can edit the push notification schedule:

  1. Click on the settings button shaped like a gear.
  2. Navigate to the Surveillance section on the left hand side and click it to expand the list if it has not been expanded.
  3. Click on Push and a view of the push notification section will appear.
  4. Click on Schedule and the push notification schedule will appear.

To edit specific times of the schedule:

  1. Choose whether or not you want to enable or disable a specific time by clicking on the corresponding button. Either Enable or Disable
  2. Click on the specific timeslot you want to edit.
  3. Click on Save to ensure changes are saved.

4.2 Phone

Click on push notifications in the settings menu to navigate to the push notifications section of the app.

To change the schedule that dictates when the user will receive push notifications:

  1. Click on Schedule
  2. Click on the specific day you want to edit
  3. Click on the specific timeslot you want to change
  4. Set that timeslot to enabled or disabled
  5. Click Save

To change the interval (the time between push notifications if the camera keeps detecting something) at which push notifications will be sent if a car has been detected:

  1. Click on interval
  2. Change interval to one of the selected options

5 Editing the Non - Detection Zone

The Non-Detection Zone is the gray area shown overlayed on top of the camera feed. Anything in the Non-Detection Zone will not trigger camera alarms, this is useful to prevent false alarms where someone might just be moving a car or walking in a section that isn't important.

5.1 Desktop

To navigate to Non-Detection Zone editing section:

  1. Click on the settings button shaped like a gear.
  2. Navigate to the Device section on the left hand side and click it to expand the list if it has not been expanded.
  3. Click on Motion Detection and a view of the Motion Detection section will appear.
  4. Click on Non-Detection Zone and the Non-Detection Zone editing section will appear.

To edit a section of the Non-Detection Zone

  1. Click on either the Paintbrush icon to be able to fill in more of the Non-Detection Zone or the Eraser icon to remove some of the Non-Detection Zone
  2. Click on the overlay to make changes, like using MS paint.
  3. Click Save to keep any changes made

5.2 Phone

To navigate to the Non-Detection Zone menu:

  1. Click on Detection Alarm
  2. Click on Non-Detection Zone
  3. Edit the not-detection zone like you would in the desktop app
